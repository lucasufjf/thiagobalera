Thiago Balera is known for his use of a "loopstation" pedalboard in his performances, which allows him to create live arrangements and backing vocals, making him a true "one man band". His repertoire includes both national and international classics, as well as contemporary hits, from artists such as Jason Mraz, John Mayer, Michael Jackson, The Beatles, Elvis Presley, Maroon 5, Jack Johnson, Oasis, Lulu Santos, Ed Motta, Jota Quest, Noel Rosa, Cartola, Chico Buarque, Djavan, and more.
